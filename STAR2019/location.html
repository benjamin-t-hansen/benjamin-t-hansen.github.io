Location

The workshop will take place at the Bernoulliborg on the Zernike Campus of the University of Groningen. The room number is 105 (building number 5161)
The address is: Nijenborgh 9, 9747 AG Groningen.


Click here for a Google Map of the location.


The reception on the 10th will include a boat ride around the canals of Groningen. After the final talk, the group will take the bus (or bike) to the central station and then walk across the street to Rondvaartbedrijf Kool. From there we will enjoy an hour cruise around the canals.

Getting Here

Flying in: Schiphol Airport and Groningen Airport Eelde (Bremen or Eindoven are possible but with a long commute)

From Schiphol Airport: An intercity train runs approximately every 30 minutes to Station Groningen Hoofdstation (Groningen Station). Tickets may be purchased at the train station. See NS for more information.

From Station Groningen to Bernoulliborg: Buses 15 runs regularly. You will get off at the stop called Nijenborgh.

There are several other buses from the city center to Zernike. Please consult 9292.nl regarding routes and fares.

Renting a bike is also possible!
