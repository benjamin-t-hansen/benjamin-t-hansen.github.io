STAR Workshop on Random Graphs 2019.

Home | Abstracts | Programme | Location

The workshop will take place April 10-12th, 2019 on the campus of the University of Groningen (location details).
This will be the 5th STAR Workshop on Random Graphs. The previous workshops took place in 2012, 2015, 2017, and 2018.


The workshop is open to all and free of charge, however registration is required to ensure sufficient catering.

The deadline for registration is April 3rd. Registration can be done by emailing Ben Hansen at b.t.hansen@rug.nl.
